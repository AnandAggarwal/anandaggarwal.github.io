About Me

I am a strong, multidisciplinary student with a wide range of interests. Whilst being a current Chemistry student at the University of Bath, I share a similarly strong interest in Data Science and, as a result, am taking up an Industrial Placement at Kindeva Drug Delivery as a Data Scientist in July 2022.

I maintain a strong interest in education, particularly in social mobility. I tutor some of the most disadvantaged pupils at KS4 through the National Tutoring Programme and, after a 15-week scheme of tutoring, I have been shown to have boosted attainment by 35%.
I have taken this interest further and sit as the Vice-Chair of a Local Governing Board for a primary school and am also an independent trustee at my university’s Students’ Union. I have used my strong understanding of data to support monitoring in both of those roles.
